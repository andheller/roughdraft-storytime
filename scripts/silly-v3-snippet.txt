[excited] The Nutville Library had a problem with returns. Not ordinary returns. Not one-book-under-the-couch returns. An avalanche of overdue books!

Miss Maple pushed up her glasses. [whispers] "This is not a library problem anymore. This is a book emergency."

Then the cookbook sneezed flour, the atlas unfolded itself across the floor, and one very small poetry book shouted, [laughs] "I demand a wagon!"
