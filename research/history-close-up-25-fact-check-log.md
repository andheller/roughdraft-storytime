# History Close-Up: 25 Event Fact-Check Log

This log tracks the verification pass for the 25-event research and draft packet. It is not a bibliography. It is a working record of what was checked, what remained intentionally cautious, and where the prose was tightened to avoid overclaiming.

## Overall Status

- All 25 entries were reviewed against the research packet and a second-pass historical plausibility check.
- No major event-level contradictions were found between the research packet and the draft file.
- The main corrections were wording corrections: reducing overstatement, clarifying scope, and adding a few concrete details where the evidence is stable.
- High-variance subjects remain intentionally cautious in the prose: Battle of Stalingrad, Reign of Terror, Wounded Knee Massacre, Gallipoli Campaign, Russian October Revolution, and Fall of Kabul.
